Our Story

Founded in 2008, Drive & Shine began as a small mobile car wash service with a simple mission: to provide exceptional car care that exceeds customer expectations. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a premier car care facility with a team of dedicated professionals.

Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and use of eco-friendly products has earned us a reputation as the go-to car wash and detailing service in the region.

Our Mission

To enhance and protect our customers' vehicles using the latest techniques and highest quality products, while providing outstanding customer service and maintaining environmental responsibility.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship in every service
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Customer satisfaction guarantee
  • Continuous improvement and innovation
