Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical service take?

Service times vary by package. Exterior washes take 30-45 minutes, while full details can take 2-4 hours.

Do I need to make an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to guarantee your preferred time slot.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes! We use biodegradable, water-based products that are safe for your vehicle and the environment.
